Government Polytechnic Nainital

ACADEMIC BODY

GPN is a technology oriented organization and has its roots in strong academics where education takes on a holistic aspect. The organization encourages and nurtures a sense of all round development and education. Students are molded in the GPN way right from day one on campus. This helps them to efficiently tackle the challenges of the professional world.
The syllabus followed by GPN is closely monitored and tailor made to get the maximum out of a technological education, in terms of industry relevant education and professional prospects.
The Academic body and Advisory Council come together to monitor, revise and suggest a course of study that is in close adherence to the industry standards.
Formed by experienced members of the academia, authorities in respective subjects, industry experts and reputed educationists, the two bodies namely, the Academic Body and the Advisory Council, lay down the framework for quality education at GPN.

Goverment Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital, established in 1957, recently celebrated it’s 67th anniversary of imparting high quality education to the students of Uttrakhand. G.P Nainital is associated to Uttarakhand board of technical education Roorkee.
