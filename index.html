Welcome to Goverment Polytechnic Nainital

Welcome to Government Polytechnic Nainital District Nainital, one of the best Polytechnic College in Uttarkahnd. Here we are single-mindly focused on addressing students' needs in an increasingly competitive world. Life is not a set of instruction but a series of experience and learning process and engineering is not also apart from it.

Our aim is not only to pursue academic excellent but also motivate and empower the talent skills and ability to each student need to be identified and encouraged so that our student is able to reach at great heights. Our unique supportive system will provide access to academic mentoring career counseling hand on experience at organization.

Prinicipal's Desk

I am honoured and feel very privileged to be the Principal of Government Polytechnic Nainital. Government Polytechnic Nainital has an outstanding reputation for establishing high expectations and achieving excellent academic results. Talents too are constantly developed through sports and varied co-curricular activities. While academic excellence is our major thrust, the College is also devoted to prepare the students for life, groom them to face the challenges of tomorrow, and encourage them to be socially relevant.
